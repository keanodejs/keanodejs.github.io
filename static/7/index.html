Mandatory Assignments

During the course there are two mandatory assignment that must be approved in order for you to qualify for the exam.

The mandatory assignments are not graded, but is approved or not approved.

You can do both mandatory assignments from home or at the school.

Mandatory Assignment 1:

Time : 11/10 - 2016, 8:30 - 13:15
The assignment can be found on fronter in a folder called "Mandatory Assignment 1" in the "Elective Info" folder. The Assignment will be visible the 11/10 8:30 and should be handed in before 13:15 the same day.
You can do the assignment at the school or from home.

Mandatory Assignment 2:

In this assignment you should make an application together with a group or a person from either the Angular class (if you are in the Node.js class) or with the Node.js Class (if you are in the Angular class).

You should demonstrate that you can work with what is the curriculum in either the Angular or Node.js Classes.

The result of this assignment is also meant as beeing something you can use for your oral exam later. You will have a project that you can talk about for the first 10 minutes of the exam, and you will automatically have some examples that you can refer to when we ask you questions about the curriculum.

Hand-in: Wednesday the 14th of December on fronter
